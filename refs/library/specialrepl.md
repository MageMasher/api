## Special Forms (REPL)

These are Special Forms that are only available in a ClojureScript REPL
session.  They mainly allow you to cherry-pick individual facilities out of the
larger [`ns`][doc:special/ns] form.

This is useful in a REPL so you can quickly reference symbols, reload
namespaces if they have changed since you've started the REPL, or change your
evaluation context to a specific namespace.

You can also use the [`cljs.repl` macros][doc:library/cljs.repl] for exploring
code from the REPL.
